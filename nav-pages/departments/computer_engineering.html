Department of Computer Engineering

About the Department

The Department of Computer Engineering was established in 2007 to meet the needs of the North Maharashtra region. The department offers an undergraduate program affiliated with North Maharashtra University, Jalgaon. Here, students gain a breadth and depth of knowledge in a multitude of computing specialties and are well-prepared for both industry and academic positions. The Bachelor of Engineering program in Computer Engineering commenced from the academic year 2007-08, with a current intake capacity of 60 students. The department has consistently been among the top performers in the region and continuously strives to improve program delivery to keep graduates competitive in the job market. Our department is known for its excellent results and has seven well-equipped laboratories. We also enjoy strong interaction and support from our alumni after graduation.
