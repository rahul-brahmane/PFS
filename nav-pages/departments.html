Departments

Computer Engineering

Goals & Objectives:

  • To provide a strong foundation in computer science and engineering.
  • To prepare students for professional careers in computing.
  • To encourage research and innovation in the field of computer technology.
  • To promote ethical practices and social responsibility.

Instrumentation Engineering

Goals & Objectives:

  • To develop expertise in measurement and control systems.
  • To enhance practical skills in instrumentation technology.
  • To foster research and development in innovative instrumentation solutions.
  • To emphasize the importance of safety and reliability in engineering.

Civil Engineering

Goals & Objectives:

  • To equip students with the skills to design and construct infrastructure projects.
  • To promote sustainable engineering practices.
  • To develop leadership qualities in project management.
  • To encourage community engagement and service through engineering projects.

Mechanical Engineering

Goals & Objectives:

  • To provide comprehensive knowledge in mechanical systems and design.
  • To promote hands-on experience through labs and projects.
  • To foster innovation and creativity in engineering solutions.
  • To prepare graduates for careers in various sectors of mechanical engineering.

Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering

Goals & Objectives:

  • To develop a strong foundation in electronics and communication systems.
  • To enhance skills in circuit design and telecommunication technologies.
  • To encourage research and development in emerging technologies.
  • To prepare students for diverse career opportunities in electronics.

Electrical Engineering

Goals & Objectives:

  • To provide a solid understanding of electrical systems and machinery.
  • To promote innovation in power systems and energy management.
  • To develop problem-solving skills for electrical engineering challenges.
  • To prepare students for careers in both traditional and renewable energy sectors.
